Exercise 4

(unsure of)

Although 'α' is not a variable of L and 'β' is not an individual symbol of L, there are α, β, and φ such that 'α' is a variable of L, 'β' is an individual constant of L, 'φ' is a formula of L, and 'φ α / β' is the same as φ.
